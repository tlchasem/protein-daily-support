iOS & iPadOS App

Protein Daily

Protein Daily is a simple and intuitive way to track your protein intake throughout the day. Designed for iPhone and iPad, the app helps you stay consistent with your nutrition goals by making tracking fast and effortless.

Features

  • Quick protein entry throughout the day
  • Automatic daily total calculation
  • Clean and distraction-free design
  • Built specifically for iOS and iPadOS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log protein?

Simply enter the amount of protein consumed and the app will update your daily total automatically.

Can I edit or delete entries?

Yes. Tap on an entry to modify or remove it.

Is my data private?

Your data remains on your device and is not sold or shared.

Compatibility

Protein Daily is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS and iPadOS.
